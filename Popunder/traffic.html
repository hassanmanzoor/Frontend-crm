Ad reach

You can easily evaluate approximate amount of visitors (traffic) you can get. Estimated volume is based on today's network statistic.

In case you dont receive sufficient amount of visitors:

  1. Turn off all not necessary  targetings (like limits, schedule etc.);
  2. Buy the same visitor more than once a day, sometimes it works as good as retargeting. The visitor might have missed your ad at the first time, but noticed it at the second;
  3. Increase Max price. Our network uses second price auction principle, which means that you can set as big maximum bid as you want, and we charge you with sufficient amount for winning in the auction. For example, if your bid is $1 up to $50, and your competitors price is $1.5, youll buy visitors for $1,55. If there are no competitors for this visitors, youll only pay $1;
  4. Please contact our support team via tickets system: we are ready to help you resolve any issue.
